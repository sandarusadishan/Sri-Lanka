Explore Our Majestic Waterfalls

Diyaluma Falls

Diyaluma Falls is the second highest waterfall in Sri Lanka, offering stunning views and natural pools.

Bambarakanda Falls

Bambarakanda Falls is the tallest waterfall in Sri Lanka, surrounded by lush greenery and hiking trails.

Ravana Falls

Ravana Falls is a popular tourist attraction, known for its cascading waters and mythological significance.

Laxapana Falls

Laxapana Falls is one of the most picturesque waterfalls in Sri Lanka, perfect for photography and picnics.

Bopath Falls

Bopath Falls is known for its unique shape resembling a leaf, and is a great spot for a refreshing dip.

St. Clair's Falls

St. Clair's Falls, also known as the "Little Niagara of Sri Lanka," is a beautiful twin waterfall.
