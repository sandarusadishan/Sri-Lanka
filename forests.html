Explore Our Lush Forests

Sinharaja Forest Reserve

Sinharaja Forest Reserve is a UNESCO World Heritage site, known for its rich biodiversity and dense rainforest.

Knuckles Mountain Range

Knuckles Mountain Range offers stunning landscapes, diverse flora and fauna, and challenging hiking trails.

Horton Plains

Horton Plains is a beautiful plateau with unique vegetation, wildlife, and the famous World's End viewpoint.

Wilpattu National Park

Wilpattu National Park is known for its dense forests, numerous lakes, and diverse wildlife, including leopards.

Yala National Park

Yala National Park is famous for its wildlife, including elephants and leopards, and its beautiful forested areas.

Kanneliya Forest Reserve

Kanneliya Forest Reserve is a biodiversity hotspot, offering scenic trails, waterfalls, and a variety of flora and fauna.
