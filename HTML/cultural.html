Cultural Triangle of Sri Lanka

Explore Our Rich Cultural Heritage

Sigiriya

Sigiriya, also known as Lion Rock, is an ancient rock fortress and a UNESCO World Heritage site.

Polonnaruwa

Polonnaruwa is an ancient city that served as the second capital of Sri Lanka, known for its well-preserved ruins.

Anuradhapura

Anuradhapura is one of the ancient capitals of Sri Lanka, famous for its well-preserved ruins of ancient Sri Lankan civilization.

Dambulla

Dambulla is known for its cave temple complex, which is the largest and best-preserved in Sri Lanka.

Kandy

Kandy is home to the Temple of the Tooth Relic, one of the most sacred Buddhist sites in Sri Lanka.

Ritigala

Ritigala is an ancient Buddhist monastery and mountain range, known for its archaeological ruins and natural beauty.
