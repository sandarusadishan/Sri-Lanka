Explore Our Beautiful Hill Country

Ella

Ella is known for its breathtaking views, lush greenery, and the famous Nine Arches Bridge.

Nuwara Eliya

Nuwara Eliya, also known as "Little England," is famous for its cool climate and tea plantations.

Kandy

Kandy is home to the Temple of the Tooth Relic and is surrounded by scenic hills and lakes.

Haputale

Haputale offers stunning views of the southern plains and is a great spot for hiking and nature walks.

Bandarawela

Bandarawela is a peaceful town known for its pleasant climate and beautiful tea estates.

Hatton

Hatton is the gateway to Adam's Peak, a popular pilgrimage site with stunning sunrise views.
