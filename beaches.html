Explore Our Beautiful Beaches

Unawatuna Beach

Known for its golden sands and turquoise waters, Unawatuna Beach is a paradise for beach lovers.

Mirissa Beach

Mirissa Beach offers stunning sunsets and is a great spot for whale watching.

Negombo Beach

Negombo Beach is famous for its vibrant nightlife and delicious seafood.

Bentota Beach

Bentota Beach is perfect for water sports enthusiasts, offering activities like jet skiing and windsurfing.

Hikkaduwa Beach

Hikkaduwa Beach is known for its coral reefs and vibrant marine life, making it a great spot for snorkeling.

Arugam Bay

Arugam Bay is a surfer's paradise, offering some of the best waves in Sri Lanka.
